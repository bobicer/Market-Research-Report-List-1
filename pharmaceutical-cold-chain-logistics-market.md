<p><p>Pharmaceutical cold chain logistics refers to the transportation and storage of pharmaceutical products in temperature-controlled environments to maintain their integrity and efficacy. One of the primary drivers is the increasing demand for temperature-sensitive drugs, biologics, and vaccines.
