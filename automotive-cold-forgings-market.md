<p><p>Automotive cold forgings refer to the production process of shaping metal components for automotive applications through compressive forces at room temperature. There are three main types of automotive cold forgings markets: custom forging, captive forging, and catalog forging. Custom forging involves producing unique and tailor-made forgings according to specific customer requirements. Captive forging refers to forgings produced in-house by automotive manufacturers for their own use. Catalog forging involves the mass production of standardized forgings that are readily available for purchase by various automotive manufacturers.</p></p>
