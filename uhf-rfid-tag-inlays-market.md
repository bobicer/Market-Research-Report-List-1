<p><p>UHF RFID tag inlays are used for wireless identification and tracking of items. UHF dry inlays are pre-designed labels that can be stuck onto products, packaging, or documents. They are commonly used in retail, logistics, and asset management. UHF wet inlays, on the other hand, are similar but have an adhesive layer that allows them to be directly applied to surfaces. Wet inlays are often used in healthcare, access control, and manufacturing. Both types enable efficient and accurate inventory management, supply chain optimization, and enhanced security measures.</p></p>
