<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Insulin pump Accessories Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubing</li><li>Reservoir</li><li>Infusion Set</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Insulin pump accessories market includes four main types of products. Tubing is a flexible and durable tube that connects the pump to the infusion set. It allows the delivery of insulin from the pump to the body. Reservoirs are containers that hold insulin and are attached to the pump. They can be disposable or refillable. Infusion sets are devices that deliver insulin from the pump to the body. They consist of a cannula that goes under the skin and a tube to transport insulin. Other accessories may include adhesives, cases, wipes, and batteries.</p></p>
