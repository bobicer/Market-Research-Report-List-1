<p><p>Electronic tank gauges are devices used to monitor fluid level in storage tanks. There are two types of market for these gauges: wireless and wired. Wireless tank gauges use radio frequency technology to transmit data to a central monitoring system, eliminating the need for physical cabling. They provide flexibility and ease of installation. On the other hand, wired tank gauges utilize cables to transmit data, ensuring a secure and reliable connection. They are ideal for environments with potential interference or where wireless signals may not be reliable. Both types offer accurate and real-time fluid level monitoring for efficient tank management.</p></p>
