<p><strong>The Reducing Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weld-Neck Reducing Flanges</li><li>Slip-On Reducing Flanges</li><li>Threaded Reducing Flanges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reducing flanges are a type of flange used to connect pipes of different sizes. There are three main types of reducing flanges in the market. Weld-neck reducing flanges have a long neck which is welded to the pipe, providing excellent strength and leakage resistance. Slip-on reducing flanges are slipped over the pipe and then welded, providing easy installation. Threaded reducing flanges have threaded holes that allow for a secure connection without the need for welding. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
