<p><ul><li>Single Main Girder</li><li>Double Main Girder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Container gantry cranes are used for efficiently handling containers in ports and terminals. Single main girder cranes have a single bridge beam that supports the trolley mechanism, providing a cost-effective solution for light to medium container loads. On the other hand, double main girder cranes have two bridge beams, allowing for increased lifting capacity and stability while handling heavier containers. These cranes are suitable for high-volume container handling operations. Both types of cranes play a crucial role in facilitating smooth and efficient container logistics.</p></p>
