<p><p>The Focused Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) market comprises two main types: Single Mode and Multi-Mode. Single Mode SAR systems are designed to operate in a specific mode, such as spotlight, stripmap, or scan mode, providing high-resolution images of a particular target area. Multi-Mode SAR systems, on the other hand, offer the flexibility to switch between different operating modes, providing a wide coverage area with moderate resolution or a narrow coverage area with high resolution. These different market types cater to the diverse needs and applications of SAR technology users.</p></p>
