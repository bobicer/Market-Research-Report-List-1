<p><strong>The Commercial Bread Flour Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Machine Milled Flour</li><li>Stone Ground Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial bread flour is categorized into two main types: machine milled flour and stone ground flour. Machine milled flour is produced using industrial mills that remove the bran and germ, resulting in a finely ground and uniform texture. This type is commonly used in commercial baking due to its consistency and high gluten content. On the other hand, stone ground flour is obtained by grinding grains using traditional stone mills, retaining the bran and germ. It is known for its coarser texture and nuttier flavor, often favored by artisanal bakers.</p></p>
