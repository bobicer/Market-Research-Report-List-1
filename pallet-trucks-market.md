<p><p>Pallet trucks are categorized into two main market types, namely manual pallet trucks and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks are operated by human power and are best suited for smaller-scale operations. They require physical effort from the operator to lift and move pallets. In contrast, electric pallet trucks are powered by electricity and are ideal for larger-scale operations. They utilize electric motors to lift and transport pallets, offering enhanced efficiency and reducing the need for manual labor. Both types serve the same purpose of moving pallets but differ in terms of power source and scale of operation.</p></p>
