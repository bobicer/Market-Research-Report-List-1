<p><strong>The Marine Trenching Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Trenchers</li><li>Jet Trenchers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine Trenching Services refers to a market that involves the digging or cutting of trenches in the seabed for various purposes. Mechanical Trenchers are types of equipment that use blades or plows to mechanically dig trenches. They offer versatility in excavation but are limited in their ability to handle hard or rocky seabeds. On the other hand, Jet Trenchers use high-pressure water jets to cut through the seabed and are particularly effective in hard or compacted soils. Both types of trenching methods have their advantages and are utilized depending on the specific conditions of the project.</p></p>
