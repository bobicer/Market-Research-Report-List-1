<p><p>The Isolation Transformer market can be categorized into two types: Single-Phase Isolation Transformer and Three-Phase Isolation Transformer. A Single-Phase Isolation Transformer is designed to provide electrical isolation and prevent the transfer of noise or surges between the primary and secondary winding. It is commonly used in residential applications and small-scale industrial settings. On the other hand, a Three-Phase Isolation Transformer is used in larger industrial settings where a three-phase power supply is required. It ensures isolation between the primary and secondary windings and minimizes power disruptions and electrical interference.</p></p>
