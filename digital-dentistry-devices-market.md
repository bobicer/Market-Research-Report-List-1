<p><p>Digital dentistry devices refer to dental equipment and technologies that utilize digital components and imaging techniques to enhance the accuracy, efficiency, and effectiveness of dental procedures. These handheld devices capture precise 3D images of the patient's mouth, eliminating the need for traditional dental impressions.
