<p><strong>The Surface Mounted Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Mixed Process</li><li>Double-sided Mixed Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface Mounted Technology (SMT) refers to the process of mounting electronic components onto the surface of a printed circuit board (PCB). There are two main types of SMT market: Single-sided Mixed Process and Double-sided Mixed Process. </p><p>In the Single-sided Mixed Process market, electronic components are mounted on a single side of the PCB, allowing for simpler assembly and lower production costs. This type is commonly used for less complex electronic devices or products with lower component density.</p><p>In the Double-sided Mixed Process market, electronic components are mounted on both sides of the PCB, enabling a higher component density and more complex circuit design. This type is suitable for sophisticated electronic devices that require advanced functionality and compact design.</p></p>
