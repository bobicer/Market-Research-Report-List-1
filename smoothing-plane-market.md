<p><p>Smoothing planes are categorized into three main market types: metal-bodied, wooden-bodied, and transitional. Metal-bodied planes are made of cast iron or steel, providing durability and stability during use. Wooden-bodied planes, on the other hand, are crafted from wood, allowing for a lighter weight and providing a unique feel to the user. Transitional planes combine features from both metal-bodied and wooden-bodied planes, often incorporating a wooden body with a metal frog and handle. Each market type appeals to different users, offering specific benefits and preferences based on material and design choices.</p></p>
