<p><p>Extracorporeal Shock Wave Therapy (ESWT) devices are used to generate shock waves that are directed towards the body to treat various musculoskeletal conditions. The market for ESWT devices can be segmented into two types: table-top/portable ESWT and hand-push type ESWT. Table-top/portable ESWT devices are compact and can be easily transported, making them suitable for mobile therapists. On the other hand, hand-push type ESWT devices are manually operated, allowing therapists to apply shock waves directly to the affected area with greater precision. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in the medical field.</p></p>
