<p><p>The Zirconium Heat Exchanger market consists of three major types: Shell Type, Tube Type, and Others. Shell Type heat exchangers are designed with a cylindrical shell that surrounds a tube bundle, allowing the transfer of heat between two fluids. Tube Type heat exchangers, on the other hand, have a series of tubes that facilitate heat exchange. Lastly, the category of Others includes heat exchangers with unique designs, such as plate type, fin type, and plate-fin type. These various types cater to different industrial requirements for efficient heat transfer processes.</p></p>
