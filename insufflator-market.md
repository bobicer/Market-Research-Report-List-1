Manufacturers are focusing on developing technologically advanced insufflators with features like automatic pressure control, flow settings, and built-in gas heating capabilities. These advancements aim to enhance patient safety, improve surgeon ergonomics, and provide better control during laparoscopic procedures.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the growing adoption of disposable and single-use insufflators. The use of disposable insufflators eliminates the risk of cross-contamination and helps maintain sterility during surgeries. These devices also offer cost-effectiveness, as they eliminate the need for cleaning, maintenance, and sterilization, reducing the overall operational costs for healthcare providers.</p><p>Geographically, North America holds a significant share in the insufflator market, attributed to the high prevalence of chronic diseases, increasing geriatric population, and the well-established healthcare infrastructure.
