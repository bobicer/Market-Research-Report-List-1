<p><p>The Waterflow Detector market consists of two main types, the Screw Type and the Saddle Type. Screw Type Waterflow Detectors are installed directly into the water pipeline and provide accurate readings of flow rates. On the other hand, Saddle Type Waterflow Detectors are installed externally and clamped around the water pipeline using brackets, enabling easy installation and removal. Both types of detectors are used for monitoring and detecting water flow in various applications such as fire protection systems, irrigation systems, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
