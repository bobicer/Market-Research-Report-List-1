Fabric backed PVC wallpaper has a fabric layer laminated with PVC, providing a luxurious and textured appearance. Vinyl coated wallpaper has a layer of vinyl on top, enhancing its water resistance and durability. The "Others" category includes specialized PVC wallpapers such as metallic or embossed variations.
