<p><p>Laboratory masticators are widely used in scientific research and medical laboratories. There are two main types of laboratory masticators on the market, namely paddle masticators and ultrasonic masticators. Paddle masticators are mechanical devices that use rotating paddles to mix and homogenize samples. They are easy to operate and suitable for a wide range of sample volumes. On the other hand, ultrasonic masticators use ultrasonic energy to break down samples into smaller particles through cavitation. They are efficient, produce less heat, and are often used for delicate or heat-sensitive samples.</p></p>
