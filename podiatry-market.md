<p><p>Podiatry is a specialized field of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disorders and ailments related to the feet, ankles, and lower limbs. Firstly, increasing awareness about foot health and the rising prevalence of foot-related conditions, especially among the elderly population, contribute to the growing demand for podiatry services. Diabetic foot ulcers and neuropathy are common manifestations among diabetic patients, necessitating specialized foot care and treatment. This factor is expected to drive market growth as the diabetic population continues to rise globally.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing interest in sports and fitness activities has led to a surge in foot and ankle injuries, creating a need for podiatric care.
