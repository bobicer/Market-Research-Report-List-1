<p><p>ESD-safe bags are specialized packaging materials used to protect electronic components from electrostatic discharges. There are three main types of ESD-safe bags in the market: antistatic bags, dissipative bags, and metallized bags. Antistatic bags are made of low charging materials that prevent static buildup. Dissipative bags have a higher level of static protection and are designed to quickly dissipate any charges that may occur. Metallized bags have a layer of metal on the inside, creating a Faraday cage effect to shield against external electrostatic fields.</p></p>
