<p><p>Special Effects (SFX) Software market can be categorized into two types: On-premise and Cloud-based. Conversely, Cloud-based SFX software is hosted and accessed over the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for local servers.
