<p><p>The bandpass optical filter market consists of two main types: single-band and multiband filters. Single-band filters allow only one specific wavelength range to pass through, while blocking other wavelengths. They are commonly used in applications where a narrow wavelength range is required, such as telecommunications and spectroscopy. On the other hand, multiband filters allow multiple wavelength ranges to pass through simultaneously, providing more flexibility in various applications, including fluorescence microscopy and biomedical imaging. Both types of filters play crucial roles in optimizing light transmission for specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
