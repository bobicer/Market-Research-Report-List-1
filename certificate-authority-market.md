<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Certificate Authority Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extended Validation</li><li>Organization Validation</li><li>Domain Validation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Certificate Authority (CA) market is divided into different types based on the level of validation and credibility they offer. Extended Validation (EV) offers the highest level of validation, thoroughly verifying the legal existence of organizations. Organization Validation (OV) validates the organization's identity and legitimacy, while Domain Validation (DV) only confirms the ownership of the domain. EV certificates assure the highest level of trust and are recommended for websites requiring user data security. OV certificates provide moderate assurance, while DV certificates are suitable for basic encryption and web server authentication.</p></p>
