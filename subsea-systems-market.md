<p><strong>The Subsea Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subsea Production System</li><li>Subsea Processing System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea systems market consists of two types: subsea production systems and subsea processing systems. Subsea production systems are used to extract oil and gas from below the sea floor. They typically include equipment such as wellheads, trees, and flowlines. On the other hand, subsea processing systems are employed to enhance the production process by separating, compressing, and treating the extracted oil and gas subsea. These systems often include subsea separators, compressors, and pumps. Both types of systems play a crucial role in enabling efficient and cost-effective oil and gas extraction from underwater fields.</p></p>
