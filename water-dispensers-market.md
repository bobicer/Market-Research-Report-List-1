<p><p>The water dispensers market can be categorized into two types: the Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler (BWC) and the Point-of-Use (POU) market. The BWC involves using pre-packaged bottles as a water source, where the dispenser cools or heats the water for consumption. On the other hand, the POU market eliminates the need for bottled water by connecting directly to a water supply and filtering it for immediate use. Both types serve as convenient and accessible methods for accessing clean drinking water in various settings.</p></p>
