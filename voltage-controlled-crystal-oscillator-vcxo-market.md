<p><p>The Voltage Controlled Crystal Oscillator (VCXO) market includes three types: Output PECL, Output CMOS, and Output SINEWAVE. Output PECL refers to the VCXOs that produce signals in the PECL (Positive Emitter-Coupled Logic) format, which is commonly used in high-speed digital applications. Output CMOS VCXOs generate signals in the CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) format, suitable for low-power digital circuits. Output SINEWAVE VCXOs produce sine wave signals, which are commonly required in telecommunications and audio applications. These different types cater to various industries, offering diverse options for voltage-controlled crystal oscillators.</p></p>
