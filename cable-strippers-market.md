<p><p>The cable strippers market consists of two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic cable strippers are machines that are designed to strip the insulation off cables automatically, without requiring manual intervention. These machines are typically used in industries where large volumes of cables need to be processed quickly. On the other hand, manual cable strippers are handheld tools that require manual effort to strip the insulation off cables. These tools are generally used for smaller scale applications or in situations where manual flexibility is required.</p></p>
