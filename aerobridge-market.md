<p><p>Aerobridge market types can be categorized into different segments. Apron Drive Aerobridge refers to a bridge that connects directly to the apron, allowing passengers to board or disembark from the aircraft. Commuter Aerobridge specifically caters to smaller commuter planes, providing a convenient connection to the terminal. Dual Aerobridge offers two separate bridges for simultaneous boarding and disembarking. Nose-Loader Aerobridge is designed to accommodate cargo planes, allowing easy loading and unloading through the aircraft's nose. These different types cater to various aircraft and operational needs within the aviation industry.</p></p>
