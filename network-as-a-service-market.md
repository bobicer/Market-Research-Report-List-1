<p><p>Network-as-a-Service (NaaS) refers to the provision of network infrastructure and services on-demand over the internet. Within this market, we have LAN-as-a-Service (LANaaS) and WAN-as-a-Service (WANaaS) as two distinct types. LANaaS enables organizations to outsource their local area network infrastructure, including switches and routers, to a service provider. In contrast, WANaaS allows businesses to outsource their wide area network, providing connectivity and services to connect geographically dispersed locations. Both LANaaS and WANaaS offer flexible and scalable networking solutions, reducing the need for extensive hardware and maintenance costs.</p></p>
