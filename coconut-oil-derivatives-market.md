<p><p>Coconut Oil Derivatives are categorized into two market types: Coconut Oil Fatty Acid and Hydrogenated Coconut Oil Derivatives. Hydrogenated Coconut Oil Derivatives, on the other hand, are produced by the hydrogenation process, which converts liquid coconut oil into a solid form.
