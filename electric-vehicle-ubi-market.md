<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle UBI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pay-As-You-Drive (PAYD)</li><li>Pay-How-You-Drive (PHYD)</li><li>Manage-How-You-Drive (MHYD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Usage-Based Insurance (UBI) market can be classified into three types: Pay-As-You-Drive (PAYD), Pay-How-You-Drive (PHYD), and Manage-How-You-Drive (MHYD). PAYD bases premiums on the distance driven, using data collected from the vehicle. PHYD considers driving behaviors like speed, braking, and acceleration to determine insurance rates. Lastly, MHYD goes beyond driving behavior and provides real-time feedback and coaching to drivers to improve their habits. These market types utilize different data points to tailor insurance rates to individual drivers and incentivize safer and more efficient driving practices.</p></p>
