<p><p>The Electrosurgical Cautery Pencil market includes two types of pencils: hand control and foot control pencils. Hand control pencils are operated manually by the surgeon, allowing for precise control and immediate response. These pencils are commonly used in surgeries that require detailed procedures. On the other hand, foot control pencils are operated using a foot pedal, freeing up the surgeon's hands for other tasks. These pencils are typically used in surgeries that require a continuous flow of energy without interruption.</p></p>
