<p><p>Sterndrive engines refer to a propulsion system used in boats and other watercraft. There are two main types in the market: electric sterndrives and ICE (Internal Combustion Engine) sterndrives. Electric sterndrives utilize electric motors powered by batteries or fuel cells to generate thrust. They offer advantages like reduced emissions, quieter operation, and potential for energy regeneration. On the other hand, ICE sterndrives rely on traditional combustion engines, typically fueled by gasoline or diesel. While these engines are more common presently, they produce emissions and create noise pollution.</p></p>
