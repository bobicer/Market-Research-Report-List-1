<p><p>Intelligent Food Packaging refers to packaging materials that are designed to actively interact and control the surrounding environment of the food product to extend its shelf life and ensure its safety. There are several types of materials used in this market. PVDC (Polyvinylidene chloride) provides excellent oxygen and moisture barrier properties. Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol (EVOH) is highly effective in blocking oxygen and preserving the freshness of food. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) provides good impact resistance and durability. Composite materials combine various properties such as barrier, strength, and flexibility to offer optimal food packaging solutions.</p></p>
