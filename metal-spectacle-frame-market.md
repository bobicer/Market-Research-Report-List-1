<p><p>The metal spectacle frame market is divided into three types: full rim, half-rim, and rimless frames. Full rim frames have metal surrounding the entire lens, providing maximum support and durability. Half-rim frames have metal only on the top half of the lens, offering a more lightweight and trendy look. Rimless frames have no metal encasing the lens, providing a minimalistic and lightweight design. These types cater to different style preferences, comfort levels, and practicality, allowing customers to choose the frame that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
