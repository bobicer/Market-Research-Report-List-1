<p><p>Rotomolding resins are materials that are used in the process of rotational molding or rotomolding. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for rotomolded products in various industries, such as automotive, construction, and consumer goods. Rotomolding resins are eco-friendly as they are made from recycled materials and can also be recycled at the end of their life cycle, reducing waste and environmental impact.
