<p><p>Autonomous Track Geometry Measurement Systems (ATGMS) are used to monitor and assess the condition of railway tracks. These systems are available in two market types: real-time type and delay type. </p><p>Real-time type ATGMS provide instant measurements and data analysis, allowing operators to immediately detect and address any track irregularities or faults. This ensures prompt maintenance and minimizes the risk of accidents. On the other hand, delay type ATGMS record track data for a specified period and then analyze it offline. This allows for more detailed analysis and identification of long-term track trends.</p></p>
