<p><p>The wire brushes market comprises various types of brushes used for different applications. Wheel brushes are circular brushes with wire bristles used for heavy-duty cleaning and deburring. Cup brushes have a cup-shaped design and are suitable for polishing and blending surfaces. Twisted brushes have twisted wire bristles, ideal for cleaning hard-to-reach areas. Strip brushes consist of metal or plastic strips with wire bristles, commonly used in sealing applications. Lastly, there are various other types of wire brushes available, catering to specific industry needs such as tube brushes, circular brushes, or specialized brushes for woodworking or metalworking tasks.</p></p>
