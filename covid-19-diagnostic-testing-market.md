<p><ul><li>Nucleic Acid Testing</li><li>Antibody Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 diagnostic testing market includes two main types: nucleic acid testing and antibody testing. Nucleic acid testing detects the genetic material of the virus, usually through the PCR method, and is considered highly accurate in identifying current infections. On the other hand, antibody testing detects the presence of antibodies produced by the body's immune system in response to the infection. It helps determine if a person has had a past infection and developed immunity. Both types play a crucial role in screening, diagnosing, and monitoring the spread of COVID-19.</p></p>
