<p><p>Pharmaceutical testing services cover a range of market types. Raw materials testing involves the analysis of ingredients used in pharmaceutical production to ensure their quality and suitability. In-process and product release testing refers to the examination of pharmaceutical products during various stages of manufacturing to ensure they meet required standards. Finished pharmaceutical products testing involves the analysis of the final products before they are released to the market. Environmental samples testing involves evaluating the impact of pharmaceutical production on the environment. The "Others" category includes additional types of testing such as stability testing and method development/validation.</p></p>
