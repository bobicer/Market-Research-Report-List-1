<p><strong>The Continuous Heating Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary-Hearth Type</li><li>Straight-Chamber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The continuous heating furnace market includes two main types: rotary-hearth type and straight-chamber type. The rotary-hearth type furnace features a rotating hearth that moves parts through the heating process, allowing for uniform heating of the entire surface area. On the other hand, the straight-chamber type furnace has a stationary chamber where parts are heated in a linear motion. Both types of furnaces are popular in industrial settings for their efficient and continuous heating capabilities.</p></p>
