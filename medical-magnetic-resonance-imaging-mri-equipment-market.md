<p><ul><li>Superconducting Magnet</li><li>Permanent Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) equipment market can be divided into two types based on the type of magnets used: superconducting magnet and permanent magnet. Superconducting magnets use a precisely cooled superconductive material to generate a strong magnetic field, which offers high image resolution and shorter scan times. Permanent magnets, on the other hand, utilize a fixed magnetic field and are typically less expensive. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various medical facilities for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
