<p><p>The microspectrometer market consists of two main types: fully integrated microspectrometers and spectrometer units. Fully integrated microspectrometers are compact devices that combine all the necessary components, such as light sources, detectors, and spectrometer chips, into a single unit. They are self-contained and can be easily integrated into various applications. On the other hand, the spectrometer unit market refers to individual components like spectrometer chips or modules that can be incorporated into existing systems or devices for spectral analysis. Both types cater to different market needs, offering flexibility and convenience in different applications.</p></p>
