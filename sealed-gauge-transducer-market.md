Low Pressure Sealed transducers are designed to accurately measure low pressure ranges, typically below 50 psi. Mid Pressure Sealed transducers are suitable for measuring pressure in moderate ranges, typically ranging from 50 psi to 500 psi. High Pressure Sealed transducers are specifically designed to withstand extreme pressure conditions, typically above 500 psi. All three types offer reliable and accurate pressure measurement solutions for different application needs in various industries.</p></p>
