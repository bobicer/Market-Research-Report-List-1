<p><p>Nipple care products primarily come in two types: lanolin nipple cream and non-lanolin nipple cream. Lanolin nipple cream is made from the natural oil obtained from sheep's wool, known for its moisturizing and soothing properties. It helps to alleviate soreness and dryness caused by breastfeeding. On the other hand, non-lanolin nipple cream is typically plant-based or made with synthetic ingredients. It offers similar benefits but is preferred by those with lanolin allergies or those seeking vegan options. Both types aim to provide relief and support for breastfeeding mothers.</p></p>
