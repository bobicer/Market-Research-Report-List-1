<p><p>The coronary catheters market consists of various types of catheters used in medical procedures related to the heart. Aspiration catheters are used to remove blood clots from blocked coronary arteries. Coronary balloon catheters help widen narrowed arteries by inflating a balloon at the site of the blockage. Diagnostic catheters are used to assess the condition of the arteries and gather information about the heart. Guide catheters help navigate other catheters to the target location within the coronary arteries. These different types of catheters are used depending on the specific requirements of the procedure.</p></p>
