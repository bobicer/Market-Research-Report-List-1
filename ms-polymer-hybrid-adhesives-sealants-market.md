<p><strong>The MS Polymer Hybrid Adhesives & Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>One-component MS Polymer Hybrid Adhesives & Sealants are pre-mixed adhesives that can be directly applied without any additional mixing or preparation. They cure when exposed to moisture in the air, forming a strong and durable bond. On the other hand, two-component MS Polymer Hybrid Adhesives & Sealants consist of two separate components that need to be mixed together before application. They offer a longer working time as the curing process is initiated only when the two components are combined, providing flexibility for complex applications.</p></p>
