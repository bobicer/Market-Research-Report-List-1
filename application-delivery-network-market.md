<p><ul><li>Controllers</li><li>Application safety equipment</li><li>Application gateways</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Application Delivery Network (ADN) market comprises three main types of solutions. Controllers are devices or software that manage traffic and optimize network performance. They ensure efficient data flow between servers and clients. Application safety equipment focuses on securing applications and data from threats like hackers or malware, using features like firewalls and encryption. Application gateways are platforms that provide application layer services, such as load balancing, SSL acceleration, and content caching. These different types of solutions work together to enhance the speed, security, and reliability of applications delivered over a network.</p></p>
