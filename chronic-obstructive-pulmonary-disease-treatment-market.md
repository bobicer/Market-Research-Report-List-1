<p><p>Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) is a progressive lung disease that causes breathing difficulties and is often characterized by symptoms such as shortness of breath, coughing, wheezing, and chest tightness. COPD is a major cause of healthcare burden globally and is often associated with smoking, exposure to pollutants, and genetic factors. The treatment for COPD aims to manage symptoms, improve quality of life, and slow down the progression of the disease.</p><p>The primary treatment options for COPD include bronchodilators, corticosteroids, and oxygen therapy. Bronchodilators help relax the muscles surrounding the airways, making breathing easier. Corticosteroids help reduce inflammation in the airways. In severe cases, oxygen therapy is used to increase oxygen levels in the body. Additionally, pulmonary rehabilitation programs are also recommended to improve lung function and overall physical condition.</p><p>The market for COPD treatment is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Factors such as the rising prevalence of COPD, increasing geriatric population, and technological advancements in treatment options are driving market growth.
