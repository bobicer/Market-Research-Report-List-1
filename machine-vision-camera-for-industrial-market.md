<p><p>Machine vision cameras are widely used in industrial settings for tasks such as quality inspection and process control. The industrial market offers two main types of machine vision cameras: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a full 2D image, similar to a traditional digital camera, and are suited for static objects or applications requiring high resolution. Line scan cameras, on the other hand, capture images line by line and are ideal for inspecting moving objects or fast-paced production lines. Both types have their specific functions and are crucial in the industrial sector.</p></p>
