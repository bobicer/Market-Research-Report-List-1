<p><p>Gearbox adapters are devices that enable the connection between the engine and the transmission system. There are three main types of gearbox adapters: manual, automatic, and semi-automatic. </p><p>Manual gearbox adapters are used in vehicles with manual transmissions, allowing the driver to manually shift gears using a clutch pedal and gear stick. </p><p>Automatic gearbox adapters are designed for vehicles with automatic transmissions, which shift gears automatically without any driver input. </p><p>Semi-automatic gearbox adapters combine features of both manual and automatic adapters, offering the option for the driver to manually shift gears or let the system shift gears automatically.</p></p>
