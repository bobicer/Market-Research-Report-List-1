<p><ul><li>Electrothermal Membrane Heating</li><li>Carbon Crystal Heating</li><li>Heating Cable Floor Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric floor heating is a type of heating system that uses electricity to warm the floors of a room. There are three main types of electric floor heating systems: electrothermal membrane heating, carbon crystal heating, and heating cable floor heating. Electrothermal membrane heating involves a thin membrane that is installed under the floor covering, which heats up when electricity is passed through it. Carbon crystal heating uses carbon crystals embedded in a panel that generate heat when electricity is conducted. Heating cable floor heating involves electric heating cables that are installed under the floor surface, which heat up when electricity is supplied to them.</p></p>
