<p><p>The metal tag embossing machines market consists of two types: manual embossing and automatic embossing. Manual embossing machines require human intervention to operate, where the user manually input the required information into the machine and embosses the metal tags one by one. On the other hand, automatic embossing machines operate independently, with pre-programmed instructions to emboss metal tags automatically without human intervention. These machines enhance efficiency and productivity by reducing the need for manual labor and increasing output.</p></p>
