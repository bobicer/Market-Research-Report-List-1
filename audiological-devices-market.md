<p><p>The market for audiological devices can be categorized into four types: hearing aids, measurement and testing devices, cochlear implants, and others. Hearing aids are electronic devices used to amplify sounds for individuals with hearing loss. Measurement and testing devices are used to assess an individual's hearing abilities. Cochlear implants are surgically implanted devices that bypass damaged parts of the ear to provide direct electrical stimulation to the auditory nerve. The "others" category includes devices such as bone-anchored hearing aids, middle ear implants, and assistive listening devices.</p></p>
