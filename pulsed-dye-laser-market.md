<p><ul><li>Standalone Lasers</li><li>Multiplatform Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pulsed dye laser market is classified into two types: standalone lasers and multiplatform lasers. Standalone lasers are a single device that is dedicated solely to delivering pulsed dye laser treatments. They are designed with specific settings and capabilities for optimal performance. On the other hand, multiplatform lasers integrate pulsed dye technology into a larger system that offers a variety of laser treatments. These platforms are more versatile, allowing practitioners to perform multiple procedures using different laser technologies on a single device.</p></p>
