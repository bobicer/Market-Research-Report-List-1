<p><p>The market for solar power system batteries can be categorized into three types: lithium-ion batteries, lead-acid batteries, and others. Lithium-ion batteries are lightweight, have longer lifespan, and higher energy density, making them popular for residential and commercial solar systems. Lead-acid batteries are reliable and have a low upfront cost, but they require more maintenance and have a shorter life compared to lithium-ion batteries. The "others" category includes alternative battery technologies such as flow batteries and sodium-ion batteries, which are still in the early stages of development but show potential for future use in solar power systems.</p></p>
