<p><p>Software Asset Management (SAM) is a practice that helps organizations manage their software assets effectively. There are two main types of SAM markets: On-premises and Cloud. </p><p>On-premises SAM involves implementing SAM tools and processes within the organization's infrastructure. It requires dedicated hardware and software resources, providing organizations with complete control and customization over their SAM setup. </p><p>On the other hand, Cloud SAM involves utilizing cloud-based software solutions and platforms. It eliminates the need for on-premises infrastructure and offers more flexibility, scalability, and accessibility. Organizations can access and manage their software assets from anywhere through an internet connection.</p></p>
