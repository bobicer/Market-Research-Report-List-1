<p><p>Desiccant dryer market types consist of heated dryers and heatless dryers. Heated dryers operate by using heat to remove moisture from the compressed air. This type of dryer is suitable for high-demand applications that require a quick drying process. On the other hand, heatless dryers do not use external heat sources and rely on the desiccant material to absorb moisture. These dryers are typically more energy-efficient and are commonly used in situations where the availability of a heat source may be limited or costly.</p></p>
