<p><p>Pepper powder refers to a popular spice produced from pepper berries. It comes in various types, including white pepper powder, black pepper powder, and green prickly ash powder. White pepper powder is derived from fully ripe berries that are soaked in water and then outer layers are removed. Black pepper powder is made from dried, unripe berries, which are then ground to a fine powder. Green prickly ash powder, on the other hand, is produced from the prickly ash plant and possesses a unique, numbing flavor. These diverse types of pepper powder offer different taste profiles and culinary applications.</p></p>
