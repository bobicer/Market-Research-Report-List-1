<p><p>Antifungal treatment market consists of various types of drugs that are used to treat fungal infections. Azoles are a common type of antifungal drug that inhibits the growth of fungus by interfering with its cell membrane. Echinocandins work by inhibiting the production of a certain component in the fungal cell wall. Polyenes bind to the fungal cell membrane and cause leakage, ultimately leading to the death of the fungus. Allylamines inhibit an enzyme needed for fungal growth. Other types of antifungal drugs include nucleoside analogs, flucytosine, and griseofulvin. These drugs are used based on the type and severity of the fungal infection.</p></p>
