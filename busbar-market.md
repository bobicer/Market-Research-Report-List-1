<p><p>Busbars are metallic strips or bars designed to distribute electrical power in buildings, electrical substations, and industrial facilities. They are used for efficient and simplified electrical power distribution and can handle high current levels with minimal voltage drop. Busbars are typically made of copper or aluminum due to their high electrical conductivity and thermal properties.</p><p>The busbar market has been growing steadily due to increasing investments in renewable energy, rising demand for power generation and transmission, and the need for efficient power distribution infrastructure. The market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the busbar market is the increasing adoption of smart grid systems. Smart grids enable real-time monitoring, control, and optimization of power distribution, resulting in improved efficiency and reduced energy losses. Busbars play a crucial role in smart grid infrastructure by providing reliable and secure power transmission and distribution.</p><p>Another trend in the busbar market is the rising demand for busbar trunking systems. Busbar trunking systems are prefabricated power distribution systems that eliminate the need for traditional cable wiring. They offer flexibility, easy installation, and enhanced safety, making them ideal for applications in commercial buildings and industrial facilities.</p><p>Furthermore, the busbar market is witnessing growth opportunities due to the expanding electric vehicle (EV) industry. With the increasing adoption of EVs, the demand for charging infrastructure is rising.
