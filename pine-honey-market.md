<p><p>Pine honey is available in three market types: extracted honey, pressed honey, and comb honey. Extracted honey is obtained by using centrifugal force to extract honey from the honeycomb. Pressed honey involves pressing honeycomb with force, resulting in the separation of honey. In the comb honey market, honey is sold in its natural state, still contained within the honeycomb cells. Each market type offers a different experience for consumers, with extracted honey being the most common and widely available, while pressed honey and comb honey provide a more unique and natural form of honey.</p></p>
