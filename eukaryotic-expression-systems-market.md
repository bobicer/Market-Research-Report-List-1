<p><p>Eukaryotic expression systems market encompasses various types of products, including reagents, expression vectors, and competent cells. Reagents are substances used in the laboratory to enhance or enable a specific biological reaction. In the context of eukaryotic expression systems, reagents are utilized to support the efficient expression of proteins. Expression vectors are DNA molecules used to insert a gene of interest into a host cell, enabling the production of desired proteins. Competent cells are host cells that have been genetically engineered to easily uptake foreign DNA and express the introduced genes.</p></p>
