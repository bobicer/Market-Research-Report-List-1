<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The PoE Connected Lighting Market comprises two main types: Powered Device Controllers and ICs, and Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and ICs. Powered Device Controllers and ICs are responsible for controlling the lighting fixtures that receive power over Ethernet. They manage the conversion of electrical power and control signals to drive the lights. On the other hand, Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and ICs control the power delivered through the Ethernet cable to the lighting fixtures. These components ensure efficient and reliable connectivity between the power source and the lighting system.</p></p>
