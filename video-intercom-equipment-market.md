<p><p>Video intercom equipment market types can be broadly classified into two categories: analog type and IP type. Analog type video intercom systems use traditional wired connections to transmit video and audio signals. They are cost-effective and commonly found in residential buildings. On the other hand, IP type video intercom systems use internet protocol networks to transmit signals, enabling remote access and control. These systems are scalable, offer advanced features like integration with other devices, and are commonly used in commercial and larger residential complexes.</p></p>
