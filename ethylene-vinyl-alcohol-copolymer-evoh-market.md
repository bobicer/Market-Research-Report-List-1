<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Alcohol Copolymer (EVOH) is a type of plastic commonly used as a barrier material for packaging applications. The market for EVOH can be segmented based on the ethylene content (mol%) present in the copolymer. EVOH with ethylene content below 29 mol% has limited oxygen barrier properties and is ideal for applications such as cables and films. EVOH with ethylene content ranging from 29-44 mol% offers good oxygen barrier properties and is commonly used for food packaging. EVOH with ethylene content above 44 mol% provides excellent chemical resistance and is utilized for applications like automotive fuel tanks and pipes.</p></p>
