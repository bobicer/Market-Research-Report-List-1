<p><p>Facial tissue folding machines are categorized into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines are highly efficient and require minimal human intervention. They can automatically handle the entire tissue folding process, including feeding, cutting, and stacking. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention. While the feeding and cutting processes are automated, the stacking of folded tissues may require human assistance. Both types of machines serve the purpose of increasing productivity and reducing labor costs in the facial tissue manufacturing industry.</p></p>
