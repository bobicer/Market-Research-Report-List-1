<p><strong>The Data Center Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Type</li><li>Liquid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Data Center Transformer market includes two main types: Dry type and Liquid type transformers. Dry type transformers are designed to be air-cooled, making them safer and more environmentally friendly but less efficient compared to liquid type transformers. Liquid type transformers, on the other hand, use a liquid coolant such as oil or silicone to absorb and dissipate heat more effectively, resulting in higher efficiency and power density. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring reliable power supply and efficient operation within data centers, but they differ in cooling methods and performance.</p></p>
