<p><p>Analytical transmitters are electronic devices used for measuring and transmitting data about various parameters in industrial processes. The multi-parameter market refers to transmitters capable of measuring and transmitting data on multiple parameters such as temperature, pressure, pH level, etc. These transmitters offer versatility and cost-effectiveness as they can monitor multiple variables simultaneously.
