<p><strong>The Dental Healing Cap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Healing Cap</li><li>Angled Healing Cap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental healing cap market consists of two main types: straight healing cap and angled healing cap. Straight healing caps are designed with a straight connector that allows for easy and direct implant abutment connection. They are commonly used in situations where there is enough space and bone support for a straightforward implant placement. On the other hand, angled healing caps are designed with a curved connector, enabling a customized angulated abutment connection. This type of healing cap is used when the implant placement requires a specific angle due to anatomical limitations or esthetic considerations.</p></p>
