<p><p>Wireless garage door openers can be categorized into two market types: Wi-Fi based and Bluetooth based. Wi-Fi based openers connect to the internet via a home's Wi-Fi network, allowing users to control the garage door remotely from anywhere using a smartphone app. On the other hand, Bluetooth based openers use a local wireless connection between the opener and a paired device, such as a smartphone, to control the garage door within a limited range. Both types offer convenience and remote access, with Wi-Fi based openers providing wider usability and Bluetooth based openers offering localized control.</p></p>
