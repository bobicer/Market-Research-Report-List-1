<p><strong>The Weld Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Weld Scanner market comprises various types such as laser, ultrasound, and others. Laser weld scanners use laser beams to detect and analyze weld defects by measuring the reflection or diffraction patterns of the laser light. Ultrasound weld scanners employ sound waves to identify defects by analyzing the changes in the transmitted sound waves caused by irregularities in the weld. The "other" category includes different technologies like X-ray and infrared scanners, which use radiation or thermal imaging to inspect welds. These diverse scanner types cater to the different needs and requirements of the welding industry.</p></p>
