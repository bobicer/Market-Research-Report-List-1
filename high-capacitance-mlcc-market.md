<p><p>The High Capacitance MLCC (Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitor) market consists of two main types: Base Metal Electrode (BME) MLCCs and Precious Metal (PGM) MLCCs. BME MLCCs utilize cost-effective base metals like nickel or copper in their electrodes, making them suitable for general applications. On the other hand, PGM MLCCs incorporate precious metals like palladium or platinum, which offer higher reliability and stability, making them ideal for specialized industries like automotive and medical. Both market types cater to different customer needs and applications within the High Capacitance MLCC industry.</p></p>
