<p><p>Endoscope camera adapters are classified into three main types: Flexible Type, Rigid Type, and Others. Flexible type adapters are designed for endoscopes with flexible insertion tubes, allowing for easier navigation in curved or narrow areas. Rigid type adapters, on the other hand, are used with endoscopes with rigid insertion tubes for more stability and control during procedures. Other types of adapters may include specialty adapters for specific medical procedures or advanced imaging technologies. Each type of adapter serves a distinct purpose in endoscopic imaging and diagnostics.</p></p>
