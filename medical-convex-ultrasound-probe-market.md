<p><strong>The Medical Convex Ultrasound Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Wave Probe</li><li>Shear Wave Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Convex Ultrasound Probe market consists of two main types: Longitudinal Wave Probes and Shear Wave Probes. Longitudinal Wave Probes emit sound waves that travel parallel to the direction of wave propagation. They are commonly used for diagnostic imaging, providing detailed internal images of organs and tissues. On the other hand, Shear Wave Probes generate waves that move perpendicular to the direction of propagation, allowing for the assessment of tissue stiffness. Both types play a crucial role in medical imaging, enabling healthcare professionals to diagnose and monitor various conditions accurately.</p></p>
