<p><ul><li>Cloud-Based Inventory Software</li><li>On-Premise Inventory Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inventory software is available in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based inventory software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, eliminating the need for businesses to install or maintain the software locally. This type offers the advantages of flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. On the other hand, on-premise inventory software is installed and operated on a business's own servers, providing more control and data security but requiring higher upfront investment and maintenance. Ultimately, the choice between the two depends on the specific needs and preferences of the business.</p></p>
