<p><ul><li>Gate Level</li><li>Quantum Annealing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantum computers market can be classified into two main types: gate level and quantum annealing. Gate level quantum computers use quantum gates to process information, similar to classical computers using logic gates. These computers can perform complex calculations and simulations but require error correction to maintain accuracy. On the other hand, quantum annealing computers focus on solving optimization problems by finding the lowest energy state within a given solution space. They offer faster optimization solutions but have limited applicability compared to gate level quantum computers.</p></p>
