<p><p>Traffic Variable Message Signs (VMS) are electronic displays used to provide real-time information to drivers on roadways. There are two main market types for VMS: fixed type and mobile type. </p><p>Fixed type VMS are permanently installed along roadways and are typically used to display important information such as traffic congestion, construction updates, or weather conditions. They are strategically placed at specific locations and provide continuous messaging to guide drivers.</p><p>Mobile type VMS, on the other hand, are portable and can be relocated as needed. They are commonly used for temporary events, construction zones, or emergencies to provide dynamic messages and direct traffic accordingly. These signs offer flexibility as they can be easily moved to different locations based on changing needs.</p></p>
