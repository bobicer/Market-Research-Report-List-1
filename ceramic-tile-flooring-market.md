<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The ceramic tile flooring market can be categorized into three types: glazed, unglazed, and lapped. Glazed tiles have a protective layer of liquid glass applied to their surface, offering a wide range of designs and colors while providing stain and slip resistance properties. On the other hand, unglazed tiles have a natural finish with no additional coating, making them more suitable for high-traffic areas due to their durability and slip resistance. Lastly, lapped tiles have a raised lip or edge, allowing the tiles to interlock and creating a seamless flooring surface, commonly used in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
