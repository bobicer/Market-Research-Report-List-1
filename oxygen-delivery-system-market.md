<p><p>The oxygen delivery system market comprises various types of devices used to deliver oxygen to patients. Oxygen masks are widely used and cover the nose and mouth to deliver a high concentration of oxygen. Nasal cannulas are used to deliver oxygen via two small tubes inserted into the nostrils. Venturi masks have adjustable valves to control oxygen concentration. Non-rebreather masks have one-way valves to prevent exhaled air re-entry. Bag valve masks are manually operated devices for assisting ventilation. CPAP masks provide continuous positive airway pressure. Other devices include tracheostomy masks, oxygen hoods, and hyperbaric oxygen chambers.</p></p>
