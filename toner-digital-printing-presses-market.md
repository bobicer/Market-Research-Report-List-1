<p><strong>The Toner Digital Printing Presses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Toner Presses</li><li>Liquid Toner Presses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Toner digital printing presses are classified into two main types: dry toner presses and liquid toner presses. Dry toner presses use a fine powder toner that is heated and fused onto the paper to create the desired image. They are commonly used for small to medium print runs and offer high-quality prints with sharp details. On the other hand, liquid toner presses use a liquid toner that is applied to the paper and then dried. They are typically used for larger print runs and offer faster printing speeds while maintaining excellent print quality.</p></p>
