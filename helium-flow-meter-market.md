<p><p>The helium flow meter market can be categorized into three types: hydraulic, thermal, and others. Hydraulic flow meters measure the flow rate of helium by utilizing the principle of hydraulic resistance. Thermal flow meters determine the flow velocity by measuring changes in helium's thermal properties. Lastly, the "others" category includes various types of flow meters that use different principles or technologies to measure helium flow rate. These classifications help differentiate the different methodologies used in helium flow measurement, allowing for a more comprehensive market analysis.</p></p>
