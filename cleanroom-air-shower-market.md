<p><p>Cleanroom air showers are an essential component in maintaining high levels of cleanliness within controlled environments. Two common types of cleanroom air showers are those equipped with HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air) filters and those with ULPA (Ultra-Low Particulate Air) filters. HEPA filters have a minimum particle removal efficiency of 99.97%, while ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency, typically removing particles down to 0.12 microns. Both types are designed to remove contaminants from individuals entering or exiting cleanrooms, ensuring the integrity of the controlled environment and protecting sensitive equipment and products from contamination.</p></p>
