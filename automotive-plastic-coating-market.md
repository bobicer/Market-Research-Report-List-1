<p><strong>The Automotive Plastic Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Base Coat</li><li>Clearcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive plastic coating market consists of three main types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. Primer is used as the initial layer to promote adhesion between the plastic surface and subsequent coatings. Base coat provides color and coverage, while also enhancing durability. Clearcoat is a transparent layer applied over the base coat to protect and provide gloss. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall performance and appearance of the coating system, ensuring long-lasting protection for automotive plastics.</p></p>
