<p><strong>The Corporate Performance Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corporate Performance Management System (CPMS) can be classified into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CPMS is a software solution provided over the internet, allowing organizations to access and manage their performance data from any location. It offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, as it eliminates the need for on-site infrastructure. On the other hand, on-premises CPMS is installed and hosted within an organization's own infrastructure, providing data control and security. This type requires higher upfront costs and maintenance but offers greater customization and control over data.</p></p>
