<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Mixed Signal System-on-Chip (MxSoC) market can be categorized into two types: standard cell-based MxSoCs and embedded design-based MxSoCs. Standard cell-based MxSoCs refer to the integration of mixed signal components onto a single chip using pre-designed standard (or generic) cells. This approach offers flexibility and customization options, as the design can be modified according to specific requirements. On the other hand, embedded design-based MxSoCs involve the creation of the entire system on a single chip, including both digital and analog components. This approach focuses on optimizing performance and reducing power consumption.</p></p>
