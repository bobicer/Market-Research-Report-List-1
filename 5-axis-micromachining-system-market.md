<p><p>The 5-axis micromachining system market includes various types of processes. Electro Discharge Machining (EDM) uses electric sparks to remove material and is suitable for intricate shapes. Electrochemical Machining (ECM) dissolves material through chemical reactions, enabling high precision. Laser Machining employs laser beams to cut or drill materials with great accuracy. Traditional Machining involves tools like drills or lathes to shape materials. These processes play vital roles in industries ranging from aerospace to medical devices, enabling precise manufacturing of components at the micro-scale.</p></p>
