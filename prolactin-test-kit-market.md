<p><ul><li>Time-resolved Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Prolactin Test Kit market consists of two main types: Time-resolved Immunofluorescence and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay. Time-resolved Immunofluorescence refers to a method where a specific molecule is labeled with a fluorescent dye and the amount of prolactin is measured based on the intensity of the fluorescence emitted. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay, on the other hand, involves the use of a chemical reaction to produce light, which is then measured to determine the concentration of prolactin. Both methods are commonly utilized in the market to accurately detect and measure prolactin levels in various medical settings.</p></p>
