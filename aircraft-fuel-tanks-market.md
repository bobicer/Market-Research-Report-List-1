<p><p>The aircraft fuel tanks market can be classified into two types: flexible tank and rigid tank. Flexible tanks are made of flexible materials and can be easily adjusted to fit in various aircraft configurations. They offer lightweight and space-saving solutions. Rigid tanks, on the other hand, are constructed with solid materials and provide higher structural integrity. They are suitable for larger aircraft and offer better fuel containment capabilities. Both types cater to different aircraft requirements and provide reliable fuel storage solutions.</p></p>
