This can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, there is a growing demand for convenience and portability in cosmetic products, especially among on-the-go consumers. Single portion packaging allows for easy travel and application, which appeals to this demographic. Additionally, the rise in the awareness of hygiene and product contamination has also contributed to the increasing adoption of single portion packaging.</p><p>Furthermore, the COVID-19 pandemic has further boosted the demand for single portion cosmetic packaging. The emphasis on personal hygiene and the need for contactless usage has prompted consumers to opt for single-use packaging solutions. This trend is expected to continue even after the pandemic, driving market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the latest trends in single portion cosmetic packaging focus on sustainability and eco-friendliness.
