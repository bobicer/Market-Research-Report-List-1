<p><p>The manual tourniquet market comprises two major types: latex tourniquets and TPE tourniquets. Latex tourniquets are commonly used for temporary blood flow restriction during medical procedures. They are made of natural rubber latex and are stretchable and adjustable to provide the desired level of pressure. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) tourniquets are gaining popularity due to their advantages such as latex-free composition, reduced risk of allergic reactions, and ease of disposal. These tourniquets offer comparable performance to latex ones while addressing concerns related to latex allergies.</p></p>
