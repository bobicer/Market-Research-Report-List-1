<p><p>The RF GaN market includes three main types: GaN-on-SiC, GaN-on-silicon, and GaN-on-diamond. GaN-on-SiC involves the integration of gallium nitride (GaN) with silicon carbide (SiC), offering high power density and efficiency. GaN-on-silicon combines GaN with conventional silicon substrate, providing a cost-effective solution with good RF power performance. GaN-on-diamond involves using a diamond substrate, which enables excellent thermal management and high-frequency operation. Each type serves specific applications in the RF market, offering a range of performance capabilities and cost considerations.</p></p>
