<p><p>The Flow Divider Market comprises two main types: Volumetric Flow and Mass Flow. Volumetric Flow refers to the measurement of the volume of fluid passing through a specific point per unit of time. It is widely used in industries that require accurate volume calculations, such as water treatment plants. On the other hand, Mass Flow measures the mass of a fluid passing through a particular point per unit of time, commonly used in industries where the weight of fluid matters, such as chemical processing. Both types have distinct applications based on the specific needs of different industries.</p></p>
