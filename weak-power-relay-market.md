<p><strong>The Weak Power Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Open Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed type weak power relays are designed to provide protection to the contacts from dirt, dust, moisture, and other external factors. These relays feature a sealed enclosure that prevents any interference or damage to the internal components. On the other hand, open type weak power relays do not have any enclosure, leaving the contacts exposed. While this makes them more vulnerable to environmental elements, they are less expensive and easier to access for maintenance. Ultimately, the choice between closed and open type relays depends on the specific requirements and operating conditions.</p></p>
