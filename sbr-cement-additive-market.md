<p><strong>The SBR Cement Additive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SBR Latex</li><li>SBR Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SBR cement additive market consists of two types: SBR Latex and SBR Powder. SBR Latex is a water-based emulsion that enhances the adhesive properties of cement and improves its flexibility, durability, and resistance to water and chemicals. It is commonly used in construction applications like waterproofing, bonding agents, and repair mortars. On the other hand, SBR Powder is a powdered form of SBR Latex, which is typically mixed with cement to enhance its mechanical properties, such as adhesion, flexibility, and impact resistance. It is mainly used in applications like floor toppings, tile adhesives, and renderings.</p></p>
