<p><strong>The Automotive Photoelectric Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photodiode</li><li>Phototransistor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive photoelectric detector market consists of various types of detectors, including photodiodes, phototransistors, and others. Photodiodes are semiconductor devices that convert light into current and are commonly used for detecting light intensity or distance. Phototransistors, on the other hand, amplify the current produced by photodiodes, making them suitable for low-light conditions. The category of "others" includes different types of photoelectric detectors, such as photovoltaic cells and photointerrupters. These detectors play a crucial role in automotive applications, enabling functions like automatic headlights, rain sensors, and obstacle detection systems.</p></p>
