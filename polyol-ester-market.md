<p><p>Polyol esters are a class of synthetic lubricants widely used in various industries. The market for polyol esters can be categorized based on different types such as NPG (Neopentyl Glycol), TMP (Trimethylolpropane), PE (Pentaerythritol), and DiPE (Dipentaerythritol). NPG-based polyol esters offer good oxidative stability, low volatility, and excellent lubricity. TMP-based polyol esters exhibit high thermal and oxidative stability, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. PE-based polyol esters provide excellent wear protection and low temperature fluidity. DiPE-based polyol esters offer improved biodegradability and low toxicity, making them environmentally friendly. Each type of polyol ester has specific characteristics that make them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
