<p><p>Tissue flotation baths are commonly used in pathology laboratories to help prepare tissue samples for microscopic examination. There are two main types available in the market: round tissue flotation baths and rectangular tissue flotation baths. The round baths have a circular shape and can hold multiple specimen containers at once, while the rectangular baths have a rectangular shape and can accommodate larger samples. Both types utilize a heated water bath system to soften and flatten tissue samples, helping to ensure optimal viewing under a microscope.</p></p>
