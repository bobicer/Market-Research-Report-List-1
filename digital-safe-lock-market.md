<p><p>The digital safe lock market includes two main types: mechanical safe locks and electric safe locks. Mechanical safe locks rely on physical components such as levers, tumblers, and keys to secure the safe. These traditional locks offer a reliable and durable solution. In contrast, electric safe locks utilize electronic components like keypads, biometric scanners, or card readers for authentication. They provide convenience, enhanced security features, and can be easily integrated into smart home systems. Both mechanical and electric safe locks ensure the protection of valuable belongings and are widely used in residential as well as commercial settings.</p></p>
