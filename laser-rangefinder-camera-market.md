<p><p>The Laser Rangefinder Camera market comprises two main types: Telescope Laser Rangefinder and Hand-held Laser Rangefinder. On the other hand, Hand-held Laser Rangefinders are portable devices used by individuals for various applications like golfing or hunting. Both types use laser technology to emit a laser beam towards the target and calculate the distance based on the time it takes for the beam to reach the target and bounce back to the device.</p></p>
