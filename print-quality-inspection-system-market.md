<p><p>The print quality inspection system market is divided into two main types: narrow web and wide web. Narrow web print quality inspection systems are designed for small to medium-sized labels, tags, and flexible packaging applications. They ensure high accuracy and precision in detecting defects, such as color variations or print errors. On the other hand, wide web print quality inspection systems cater to larger format printing, such as signage and banners. They offer comprehensive inspection capabilities, enabling detection of imperfections across wide and continuous prints.</p></p>
