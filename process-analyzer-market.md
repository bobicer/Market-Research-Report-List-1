They are commonly used in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food and beverage.</p><p>Gas analyzers, on the other hand, analyze the composition and concentration of gases in industrial processes. They are widely used in industries like oil and gas, chemical, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
