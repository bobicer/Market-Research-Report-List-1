<p><p>Polymerized Styrene Butadiene Rubber (SBR) is available in different market types including Emulsion SBR, Solution SBR, and Others. Emulsion SBR is produced by emulsion polymerization and is known for its high abrasion resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for tire manufacturing. Solution SBR, on the other hand, is produced through solution polymerization and is commonly used in the production of industrial rubber goods. The "Others" category includes specialty SBR types tailored for specific applications such as adhesives and sealants.</p></p>
