<p><p>The dental air polisher market includes two types of polishers: table top polishers and handy polishers. Table top polishers are larger, stationary machines that are typically used in dental clinics or laboratories. They are designed for heavy-duty polishing and offer advanced features for efficient cleaning. On the other hand, handy polishers are smaller, portable devices that are easy to handle and can be used directly on patients. They are convenient for quick touch-ups or for use in smaller dental settings. Both types of polishers serve the purpose of removing stains and plaque from teeth using a stream of compressed air and abrasive powder.</p></p>
