<p><p>Transformer oil testing is essential for assessing the condition of transformer oil, which is crucial for the efficient operation of transformers. The market types of transformer oil testing include Dissolved Gas Analysis (DGA), which examines gases present in the oil to identify potential problems, Moisture Analysis that measures the water content, Dielectric Breakdown Voltage to evaluate insulation properties, Flash Point to determine oil's flammability, Interfacial Tension to assess oil's impurity level, and other tests that analyze factors like acidity, viscosity, and oxidation stability. These tests help in maintaining transformer health and prolonging its lifespan.</p></p>
