<p><p>Multiple Effect Distillation (MED) Units are used for desalination processes to convert seawater into freshwater. There are two types of MED units in the market. One is Multiple Effect Distillation with Thermal Vapour Compression (MED-TVC), which uses thermal energy to generate steam and compress it into a lower pressure stage to enhance evaporation. The other type is Multiple Effect Distillation with Mechanical Vapour Compression (MED-MVC), which utilizes mechanical energy to compress the steam and achieve a similar evaporation effect. Both technologies are effective in desalination processes and have their own unique advantages.</p></p>
