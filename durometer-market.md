Brinell durometers use a steel ball to measure hardness, while Vickers durometers utilize a special pyramid-shaped indenter. Rockwell durometers employ a cone-shaped indenter and measure hardness based on the depth of the indentation. Other durometer types exist, each using unique methods to assess hardness. These instruments cater to different industries, providing accurate and reliable hardness measurements for a variety of materials.</p></p>
