<p><p>The oil flooded rotary vane vacuum pump market includes two main types: single stage rotary vane vacuum pumps and two stage rotary vane vacuum pumps. Single stage pumps use a single set of vanes to create a vacuum, while two stage pumps use two sets of vanes to achieve higher levels of vacuum. Both types require oil for lubrication and sealing, and are commonly used in various industries for applications such as packaging, medical equipment, printing, and vacuum drying.</p></p>
