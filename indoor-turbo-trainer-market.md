<p><p>The indoor turbo trainer market can be categorized into two types: classic trainers and smart trainers. Classic trainers are basic models that use a resistance unit to simulate outdoor riding conditions. They require the rider to manually adjust the resistance level and do not have any advanced technological features. On the other hand, smart trainers are equipped with advanced technology, such as wireless connectivity and power meters. They provide an interactive and immersive indoor cycling experience, allowing riders to connect to training apps, simulate real-world routes, and track performance metrics.</p></p>

<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Indoor Turbo Trainer market?</strong></p>
