<p><p>The Industrial Infrared Scanner market encompasses various types of scanners, including Standard Scanners and Stereo Scanners. Standard Scanners are commonly used in industrial applications and provide single-point temperature measurements. They are simple and cost-effective solutions for temperature monitoring. On the other hand, Stereo Scanners offer a more advanced capability by capturing thermal images from two different angles, allowing for 3D representation and enhanced temperature analysis. These scanners are typically used in complex industrial environments that require more precise thermal imaging and temperature measurement capabilities.</p></p>
