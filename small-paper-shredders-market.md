<p><p>The small paper shredders market is categorized into three types: cross cut, strip cut, and others. Cross cut shredders slice paper into tiny particles, offering a higher level of security as it is difficult to piece them back together. Strip cut shredders, on the other hand, cut paper into long, thin strips, making it easier to reassemble the document. The "others" category typically includes micro-cut shredders that provide the highest level of security, cutting paper into even smaller particles. These shredders cater to different security requirements and user preferences.</p></p>
