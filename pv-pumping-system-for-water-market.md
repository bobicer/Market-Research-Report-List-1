<p><p>A PV pumping system for water is a system that uses solar energy to power pumps that are used for water supply. There are two main types of pumps in the market - submersible pumps and surface pumps. Submersible pumps are installed beneath the water surface and are commonly used for deep wells or boreholes. Surface pumps, on the other hand, are installed above ground and are used for shallow wells or surface water sources. Both types of pumps are powered by solar energy, making them cost-effective and environmentally friendly options for water supply.</p></p>
