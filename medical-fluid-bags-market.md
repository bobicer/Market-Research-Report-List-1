Drain bags collect bodily fluids during surgical procedures or for patients with urinary catheters. Enema bags are used for administering liquid into the rectum. Biohazard disposable bags are used to safely dispose of medical waste. Breast milk bags are used for storing and transporting breast milk. Reagent bags hold chemical substances for diagnostic testing. Dialysis bags are used in kidney dialysis treatments.</p></p>
