<p><p>Digital radiography in chest radiography refers to the use of digital technology to capture and process X-ray images of the chest. Two types of systems available in the market are the CR Tech Digital X-Ray System and DR Tech Digital X-Ray System. CR Tech utilizes a cassette-based system where the X-ray image is captured on a phosphor plate and then processed digitally. On the other hand, DR Tech directly captures the X-ray image using a digital detector and eliminates the need for cassettes, allowing for faster image acquisition and processing.</p></p>
