<p><p>Two-way radios are communication devices used to transmit and receive audio signals. Analog two-way radios use traditional radio wave technology to transmit and receive audio signals and are generally less expensive. On the other hand, digital two-way radios use advanced technology to convert audio signals into a digital format, providing clearer and more secure communication with additional features such as text messaging and GPS tracking.
