<p><p>The Active Infrared Detector market is segmented into two major types: Thermal Type and Quantum Type. Thermal Type detectors function by detecting the heat emitted by an object or individual. They are effective in detecting motion and temperature changes. Quantum Type detectors, on the other hand, are based on the principles of quantum physics. They utilize the interaction between light particles (photons) and matter to detect objects or movements. These detectors are highly sensitive and can detect even the slightest changes in infrared radiation.</p></p>
