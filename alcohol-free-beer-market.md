<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alcohol-free beer can be produced using two main methods: fermentation-limited method and dealcoholization method. In the fermentation-limited method, the alcohol content is limited during the brewing process, resulting in a lower alcohol content in the final product. On the other hand, the dealcoholization method involves removing the alcohol from regular beer after it has been brewed. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences, with the fermentation-limited method offering a more natural taste while the dealcoholization method may alter the flavor slightly.</p></p>
