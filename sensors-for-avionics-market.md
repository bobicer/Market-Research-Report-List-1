<p><p>The avionics market encompasses various sensor types crucial for aircraft operations. Temperature sensors monitor engine or cabin temperature, aiding in system control. Pressure sensors provide accurate air pressure readings for altitude control and weather analysis. Motion sensors detect aircraft movements, ensuring stability and safety. Position sensors determine an aircraft's position, speed, and direction. Image sensors capture visual data for navigation and surveillance purposes. Additionally, there are other sensors like flow sensors, acceleration sensors, and proximity sensors used for specific aircraft functionalities. These sensors collectively enhance flight safety, navigation, and control, improving overall avionic performance.</p></p>
