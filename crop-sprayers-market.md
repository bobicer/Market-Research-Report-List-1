<p><p>Crop sprayers are used in agricultural practices for the effective application of pesticides, fertilizers, and other substances. The market is categorized into three types based on the volume of spray applied per hectare. Ultra Low Volume (ULV) sprayers use less than 5 liters of spray per hectare, ideal for targeted applications in smaller areas. Low Volume (LV) sprayers apply 5 to 400 liters per hectare, suitable for a wide range of crops. High Volume (HV) sprayers, applying more than 400 liters per hectare, are preferred for large-scale farming operations that require extensive coverage.</p></p>
