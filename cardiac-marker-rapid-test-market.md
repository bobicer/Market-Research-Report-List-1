<p><p>The Cardiac Marker Rapid Test market consists of various types of tests to diagnose heart-related conditions. Troponin, CK-MB, Myoglobin, and other tests are commonly used markers. Troponin is a protein released during a heart attack. CK-MB measures an enzyme released during heart muscle injury. Myoglobin detects oxygen depletion in heart muscles. Other markers may include B-type natriuretic peptide (BNP) and C-reactive protein (CRP), which indicate heart stress and inflammation, respectively. These tests help healthcare professionals quickly identify cardiac issues, allowing for effective and timely treatment.</p></p>
