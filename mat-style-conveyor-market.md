<p><p>The Mat-Style Conveyor market offers two main types: Rubber Belt and Thermoplastic Belt. Rubber Belt conveyors are durable, flexible, and resistant to abrasion, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Thermoplastic Belt conveyors are lighter in weight, provide quieter operation, and offer greater flexibility in design. Both market types cater to different industries and applications, with Rubber Belt conveyors commonly used in mining, agriculture, and manufacturing, while Thermoplastic Belt conveyors are often employed in food processing, packaging, and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
