<p><p>The hydrogen gas onsite generator market includes traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers use an alkaline solution as the electrolyte to produce hydrogen gas through the process of electrolysis. On the other hand, PEM electrolyzers employ a solid proton-conducting polymer membrane as the electrolyte. Both types of electrolyzers are used to generate hydrogen gas onsite, allowing for efficient and sustainable production without the need for transportation or storage.</p></p>
