<p><p>Medical imaging outsourcing involves the practice of delegating diagnostic tasks such as Computed Tomography (CT), Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), and Positron Emission Tomography (PET) to external service providers. CT scanning employs X-ray technology to produce cross-sectional images of the body. MRI uses powerful magnets and radio waves to generate detailed images of organs and tissues. PET scans involve injecting a specialized radioactive substance into the body to produce images of metabolic processes. These outsourcing market types enable healthcare providers to access advanced imaging services, reduce costs, and improve patient care.</p></p>
