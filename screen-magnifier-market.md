<p><p>Screen Magnifier, also known as a computer screen magnifier or desktop magnifier, is assistive software or hardware that enlarges the content displayed on a computer screen. Screen magnifiers provide various features such as zooming capabilities, color contrast adjustments, screen tinting, or highlighting specific areas for easier visibility.</p><p>The Screen Magnifier Market is projected to see substantial growth during the forecast period.
