<p><p>The medical ligating clips market consists of two main types: polymer type and metal type. Polymer type ligating clips are made of materials such as polycarbonate or polylactic acid and are generally used for temporary ligation during surgical procedures. They offer advantages like radiolucency and lower cost. On the other hand, metal type ligating clips are made of materials like titanium or stainless steel and are used for permanent ligation. They provide better longevity and strength but may be more expensive. Both types play crucial roles in various medical procedures.</p></p>
