<p><p>Piezoelectric actuators are devices that convert electrical energy into mechanical motion or force by utilizing the piezoelectric effect. There are different types of piezoelectric actuators available in the market. Stack actuators consist of multiple thin piezoelectric layers stacked together, allowing for high displacement and force capabilities. Stripe actuators, on the other hand, are thin piezoelectric plates that generate motion in a linear direction. Both types of actuators find applications in various industries, including precision positioning, robotics, aerospace, and medical devices.</p></p>
