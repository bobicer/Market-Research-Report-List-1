<p><p>The Industrial Floor Scrubber Market includes three main types of scrubbers: Walk-Behind Scrubbers, Ride-On Scrubbers, and Robotic Floor Scrubbers. Ride-On Scrubbers offer a seated platform for operators, allowing for larger surface coverage and increased productivity. Robotic Floor Scrubbers are self-operating machines that use sensors and AI technology to navigate and clean floors without human intervention. These types cater to different cleaning needs and environments, providing efficient and effective floor cleaning solutions.</p></p>
