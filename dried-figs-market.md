<p><p>Dried figs can be found in two main market types: processed products and natural products. These processed figs often come in various forms like slices, paste, or bars. On the other hand, natural products market comprises dried figs that are entirely natural and undergo minimal processing, retaining their natural taste, texture, and nutritional value. These figs are typically sold in their whole form.</p></p>
