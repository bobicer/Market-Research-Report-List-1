<p><p>Fire elevator market includes different types such as split type and straight type. In the split type market, the fire elevator is designed to have two separated compartments with different functions. This allows passengers to evacuate safely while firefighters can access the other compartment for fire control operations. On the other hand, the straight type market offers fire elevators with a single compartment that serves both evacuation and firefighting purposes. These different market types provide options for building owners and designers to choose the most suitable fire elevator solution for their specific needs.</p></p>
