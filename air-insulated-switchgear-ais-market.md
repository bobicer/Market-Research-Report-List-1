<p><p>Air-insulated switchgear (AIS) is a type of electrical switchgear that is primarily used for the distribution of electricity in substations. It consists of several components such as circuit breakers, disconnectors, and current transformers, among others, that help in the safe and efficient distribution of power. AIS operates in atmospheric air and does not require any additional insulation of gas or oil.</p><p>The air-insulated switchgear market has been experiencing steady growth over the years. One of the key drivers for this growth is the increasing demand for electricity in various sectors such as residential, commercial, and industrial. With the rising population and urbanization, the need for reliable and uninterrupted power supply has become crucial. Moreover, the increasing investments in infrastructure development, especially in emerging economies, have further propelled the demand for air-insulated switchgear.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing focus on renewable energy sources has also contributed to the growth of the AIS market. The integration of renewable energy systems, such as wind and solar, into the existing power grid requires the installation of switchgear to ensure efficient transmission and distribution of electricity.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing several trends that are expected to drive its growth in the future. These include the development of smart switchgears that incorporate advanced technologies like IoT, cloud computing, and automation.
