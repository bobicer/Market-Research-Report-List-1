<p><p>Endodontic handpieces are dental instruments used by endodontists during root canal procedures. Endodontic handpieces vary in design and functionality, with some offering features such as torque control, auto-reverse functions, and compatibility with rotary files.</p><p>The endodontic handpieces market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue to expand at a promising rate. Factors contributing to this growth include the rising prevalence of dental diseases and the increasing demand for minimally invasive and advanced endodontic treatments. Additionally, technological advancements in endodontic handpieces that enable efficient and precise root canal procedures are also driving market growth.</p><p>One notable trend in the endodontic handpieces market is the growing adoption of electric handpieces over traditional air-driven handpieces. Electric handpieces offer numerous advantages, such as higher torque, consistent speed, improved control, and reduced noise and vibration levels. These benefits have led to a shift towards electric handpieces among endodontists, resulting in their higher adoption rate.</p><p>The market is also witnessing the introduction of wireless and cordless endodontic handpieces, which offer greater convenience and freedom of movement during procedures.
