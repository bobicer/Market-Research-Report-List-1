<p><p>The piezo buzzers market consists of two main types: active piezo buzzers and passive piezo buzzers. </p><p>Active piezo buzzers require an external power source to produce sound. They are commonly used in applications where a loud and clear sound is necessary, such as alarms or sirens. These buzzers are versatile and offer a wide range of tones and frequencies.</p><p>Passive piezo buzzers, on the other hand, do not require an external power source and produce sound when an electrical signal is applied to them. They are simpler and more cost-effective but are limited in terms of volume and tone options. These buzzers are commonly found in small electronic devices like watches or calculators.</p></p>
