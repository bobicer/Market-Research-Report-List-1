<p><p>Rubber gym flooring comes in two main types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber flooring is derived from the sap of rubber trees and is considered more environmentally friendly. It offers excellent durability, shock absorption, and slip resistance. On the other hand, synthetic rubber flooring is made from petroleum-based materials and offers similar properties to natural rubber flooring. It is often more cost-effective and can be customized to meet specific design requirements. Both types of rubber gym flooring are popular choices due to their versatility and ability to withstand heavy use in gym and fitness settings.</p></p>
