<p><ul><li>Magnetic Detection</li><li>Contactless Switch</li><li>Proximity Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic sensitive transistors have various applications in the market, such as magnetic detection, contactless switches, and proximity switches. In magnetic detection, these transistors are used to sense and measure magnetic fields, making them suitable for applications like compasses and magnetic field mapping. Contactless switches utilize magnetic sensitive transistors to detect the presence or absence of a magnetic field, enabling their use in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics for non-contact switching operations. Proximity switches, on the other hand, utilize these transistors to detect the proximity of an object without physical contact, finding applications in security systems and automation technologies.</p></p>
