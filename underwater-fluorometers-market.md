<p><ul><li>Single Channel Fluorometer</li><li>Multichannel Fluorometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater fluorometers market consists of two main types: single channel fluorometers and multichannel fluorometers. Single channel fluorometers are devices that measure fluorescence from a single wavelength or channel, providing data on a specific parameter. On the other hand, multichannel fluorometers can measure fluorescence from multiple wavelengths or channels simultaneously, allowing for the analysis of multiple parameters at once. Both types of fluorometers are used to study the presence and concentration of various substances in underwater environments.</p></p>
