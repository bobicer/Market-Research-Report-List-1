<p><p>The protein tyrosine kinase market can be divided into two types: receptor type and non-receptor type. Receptor type protein tyrosine kinases are enzymes that are located on the cell surface and play a vital role in cell signaling. They are involved in the regulation of various cellular processes such as growth, differentiation, and metabolism. Non-receptor type protein tyrosine kinases, on the other hand, are found within the cell and are involved in intracellular signaling pathways. They are responsible for transmitting signals from cell surface receptors to the nucleus, ultimately influencing gene expression and cellular function.</p></p>
