These cans are commonly used for preserving and packaging food and beverages, providing airtight and secure packaging. In contrast, Two-Piece Cans are made from a single seamless body, eliminating the need for a separate lid. These cans are mainly used for beverages, offering convenience and cost-effectiveness in the packaging process.</p></p>
