<p><strong>The Low Pressure Molding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Auto Type</li><li>Semi-Auto Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Pressure Molding Machine market can be broadly categorized into two types: Auto Type and Semi-Auto Type. Auto Type machines are fully automated, requiring minimal human intervention. They have advanced features like automatic material feeding, mold closing, and ejection, making them efficient and ideal for high-volume production. On the other hand, Semi-Auto Type machines involve some manual operations, such as manually loading the material and removing the molded products. These machines are suitable for applications with lower production volumes or when customization is required.</p></p>
