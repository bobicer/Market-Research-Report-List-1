<p><p>The Injection Molding Machine Controller market consists of two main types: Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) and Non-Programmable Logic Controllers (Non-PLCs). PLCs are highly flexible and can be easily programmed to control various aspects of the injection molding process, including temperature, pressure, and timing. Non-PLCs, on the other hand, are simpler devices that operate based on pre-set parameters and do not offer programmability. Both types of controllers play a crucial role in regulating and optimizing the performance of injection molding machines.</p></p>
