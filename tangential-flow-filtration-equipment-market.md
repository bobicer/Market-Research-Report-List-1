<p><p>Tangential flow filtration equipment is divided into various market types: disposable systems, reusable systems, filter boxes, filter cartridges, and filter accessories. Filter boxes are compact, easy-to-use units that contain a filtration membrane. Filter cartridges are single-use units that can be easily replaced. Filter accessories include various components and tools used in the filtration process, such as tubing, connectors, and fittings.</p></p>
