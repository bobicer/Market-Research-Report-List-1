<p><p>The Epistaxis Device Market consists of three main types: nasal tampons, nasal catheters, and others. Nasal tampons are soft, absorbent materials placed inside the nostrils to apply pressure and stop bleeding. Nasal catheters, on the other hand, are inserted into the nasal cavity to deliver medications or apply direct pressure to control bleeding. The "others" category includes alternative devices not included in the aforementioned types, such as nasal dressings or nasal packing materials. All of these devices aim to provide effective solutions for managing epistaxis (nosebleeds).</p></p>
