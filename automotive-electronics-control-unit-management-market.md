<p><p>In the automotive industry, Electronic Control Unit (ECU) management is crucial for efficient vehicle performance and operation. Different types of ECUs fulfill diverse functions. Powertrain Control Module (PCM) manages engine performance, fuel, and emissions control. Transmission Control Module (TCM) oversees the shifting of gears and clutch engagement in automatic transmissions. Central Timing Module (CTM) controls various digital features like dashboard displays and lighting systems. Body Control Module (BCM) manages functions related to the car's body, such as windows, locks, and mirrors. Other ECUs cater to specific needs, like the anti-lock braking system or airbag control.</p></p>
