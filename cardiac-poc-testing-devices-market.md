<p><p>Cardiac POC testing devices are used for rapid and on-site diagnosis of cardiac conditions. Troponin test devices measure the levels of troponin, a protein released during heart muscle damage, to detect heart attacks. Brain natriuretic peptide (BNP) test devices measure the levels of BNP, a hormone released in response to heart failure, helping in the diagnosis and management of heart failure. The "Others" segment of the market includes various cardiac biomarker tests for diagnosing conditions like angina, arrhythmias, and myocardial infarction, aiding in quick decision-making for timely treatment.</p></p>
