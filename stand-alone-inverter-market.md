There are two main types of stand-alone inverters based on the phase of the AC power they produce. Single-phase inverters produce AC power with a single sine wave and are commonly used in residential applications. Three-phase inverters, on the other hand, produce AC power with three sine waves and are typically used in larger commercial or industrial applications.</p></p>
