<p><p>The XLR connector market consists of two types: male and female connectors. Male connectors have three pins and are designed to be inserted into a corresponding female connector. They are usually found on cables or devices that supply signals. Female connectors, on the other hand, have three holes that receive the male pins. They are typically found on devices or inputs that receive signals. Male and female connectors provide a secure, balanced, and low-noise connection for audio and video equipment, ensuring reliable signal transmission.</p></p>
