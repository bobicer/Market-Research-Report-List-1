<p><p>Medical grade hydrogel is a type of material used in various medical applications. There are two main types of crosslinking techniques used to create medical grade hydrogels: crosslinking agent crosslinking and physical crosslinking. Crosslinking agent crosslinking involves using a chemical agent to bind the polymer chains in the hydrogel together. On the other hand, physical crosslinking relies on physical interactions, such as temperature or pH changes, to create a network structure within the hydrogel. Both crosslinking methods are employed to enhance the stability and mechanical properties of medical grade hydrogels for different medical purposes.</p></p>
