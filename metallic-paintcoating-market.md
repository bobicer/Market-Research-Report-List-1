<p><strong>The Metallic Paint/Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic paint/coating market types include solvent-based and water-based options. Solvent-based metallic paints use chemicals to dissolve the paint pigments, providing a durable and long-lasting finish. However, they can emit strong odors and are more harmful to the environment. On the other hand, water-based metallic paints use water as a carrier for the paint pigments, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up. While water-based paints may not be as durable as solvent-based options, they offer a safer and more sustainable alternative.</p></p>
