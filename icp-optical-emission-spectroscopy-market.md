<p><p>ICP Optical Emission Spectroscopy (ICP-OES) is a popular technique used to analyze the elemental composition of a variety of samples. The market for ICP-OES instruments is divided into two main types - Sequential and Simultaneous. Sequential ICP-OES analyzes one element at a time, sequentially scanning through each element's spectral lines. Simultaneous ICP-OES, on the other hand, measures all elements simultaneously using a polychromator. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice depends on the specific analytical requirements and budgetary considerations of the user.</p></p>
