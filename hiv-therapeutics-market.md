<p><strong>The HIV Therapeutics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nucleoside-Analog Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTIs)</li><li>Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTIs)</li><li>Entry and Fusion Inhibitors</li><li>Protease Inhibitors (PIs)</li><li>Integrase Inhibitors</li><li>Coreceptor Antagonists</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HIV therapeutics market offers various types of drugs to treat the virus. Nucleoside-Analog Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NRTIs) disrupt the reproduction process of the virus. Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors (NNRTIs) bind to a specific protein to inhibit viral replication. Entry and Fusion Inhibitors block the virus from entering human cells. Protease Inhibitors (PIs) prevent viral protein formation. Integrase Inhibitors inhibit the virus from integrating its genetic material into human DNA. Coreceptor Antagonists prevent the virus from entering and infecting cells. These drug types play a crucial role in managing HIV infections.</p></p>
