<p><p>The medical shoe covers market consists of two main types: waterproof and non-waterproof. Waterproof shoe covers are made from impermeable materials that prevent the penetration of liquid, such as water or bodily fluids, making them suitable for environments where there is a risk of spills or contamination. On the other hand, non-waterproof shoe covers are made from breathable materials that allow air circulation while providing a barrier against dirt and dust. These are commonly used in sterile environments where fluid protection is not a requirement.</p></p>
