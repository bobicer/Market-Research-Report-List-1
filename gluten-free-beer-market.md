<p><p>The gluten-free beer market can be divided into two main types: dedicated gluten-free beers and gluten-reduced beers. Dedicated gluten-free beers are brewed without using any gluten-containing ingredients, such as barley or wheat, and are suitable for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity. On the other hand, gluten-reduced beers are made from traditional ingredients containing gluten but undergo a specialized process to reduce the gluten content to below the recognized threshold level. While dedicated gluten-free beers are considered completely safe for those with gluten-related disorders, gluten-reduced beers may still pose a risk for some individuals.</p></p>
