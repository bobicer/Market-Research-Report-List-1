<p><p>The floor-standing automated immunoassay analyzer market comprises various types of immunoassay technologies. Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA) utilizes a chemical reaction to produce light and measure analytes. Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Immunoassay (ELFA) employs fluorescent markers to detect analytes. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) uses enzymes for analyte detection. Radioimmunoassay (RIA) employs radioactive substances to identify analytes. Additionally, there are other types of immunoassays available. All these techniques are utilized in floor-standing automated immunoassay analyzers for fast and accurate diagnostic testing in laboratories and healthcare settings.</p></p>
