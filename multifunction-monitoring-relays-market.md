<p><p>Multifunction Monitoring Relays are devices used to monitor various parameters in electrical systems to prevent damage and ensure safety. There are two main types: 1-Phase and 3-Phase. </p><p>1-Phase Monitoring Relays are designed for single-phase systems, such as residential or small commercial buildings, where the electrical load is distributed across a single phase. These relays monitor parameters like voltage, current, frequency, and power factor to detect faults or irregularities.</p><p>In contrast, 3-Phase Monitoring Relays are used in industrial or larger commercial applications where electrical systems consist of three phases. These relays monitor parameters across all three phases, ensuring balanced power distribution and detecting any abnormalities.</p><p>Both types of relays play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and reliability of electrical systems by providing real-time monitoring and protection.</p></p>
