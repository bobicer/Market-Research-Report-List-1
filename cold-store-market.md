Refrigerated storage involves storing and preserving food at controlled temperatures to prevent spoilage, suitable for items such as meat, fish, or beverages. Freezer market entails storing food at even lower temperatures, freezing it to maintain its quality for long durations, ideal for frozen products like ice cream or frozen vegetables.</p></p>
