<p><p>Inline checkweighers are utilized in industries to weigh and measure products during production or packaging processes. In-motion checkweighers allow products to move continuously on a conveyor belt while being weighed, enabling fast and efficient measurements. On the other hand, intermittent checkweighers weigh products that are stopped momentarily on the conveyor belt before proceeding. Both types help ensure product quality and accuracy by detecting underweight or overweight items. They are widely used in various sectors like food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, and logistics to improve operational efficiency and comply with quality standards.</p></p>
