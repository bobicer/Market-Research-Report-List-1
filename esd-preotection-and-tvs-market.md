<p><p>ESD (electrostatic discharge) protection refers to measures taken to prevent damage to electronic components from electrostatic discharge. It involves using devices or techniques to divert or absorb the potentially harmful discharge. TVS (transient voltage suppression) is a type of ESD protection component that prevents voltage spikes or transients from damaging sensitive electronic circuits. The TVS market includes various types of TVS devices such as metal-oxide varistors, diodes, and thyristors. These devices are used in various industries, including electronics, telecommunications, automotive, and industrial, to protect electronic devices and systems from transient voltage events.</p></p>
