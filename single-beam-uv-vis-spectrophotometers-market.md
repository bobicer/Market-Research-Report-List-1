<p><p>Single Beam UV-VIS Spectrophotometers are used in various industries for measuring the absorbance of light in a sample. Semi-automatic spectrophotometers offer some automation features, such as auto-wavelength selection, making them more efficient than manual ones. Fully automatic spectrophotometers are the most advanced, with automated functions for sample handling, data analysis, and report generation, suitable for high-throughput laboratories.</p></p>
