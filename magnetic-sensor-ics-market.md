<p><ul><li>AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance Effect)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic Sensor ICs are electronic devices that measure and detect magnetic fields. There are different types available in the market, including AMR, GMR, and TMR. AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance Effect) sensors change their resistance based on the direction of the magnetic field. GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance Effect) sensors use multiple layers to amplify the change in resistance caused by the magnetic field. TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance Effect) sensors work by measuring the tunneling current between two layers. These technologies provide a wide range of options for magnetic sensing applications.</p></p>
