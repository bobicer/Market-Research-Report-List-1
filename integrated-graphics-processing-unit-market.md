<p><p>The Integrated Graphics Processing Unit (IGPU) market can be classified into two types: Independent IGPU and Hybrid IGPU. Independent IGPU refers to a stand-alone graphics chip integrated onto the motherboard, with dedicated memory and processing power. It enables high-performance graphics and is often found in gaming or graphics-intensive applications. On the other hand, Hybrid IGPU combines a lower-power integrated graphics chip with a separate high-performance graphics card. This allows for improved power efficiency and better graphics performance, typically used in laptops and mobile devices.</p></p>
