<p><p>The rotary piston vacuum pumps market can be categorized into two types: single-stage and two-stage pumps. Single-stage rotary piston vacuum pumps have one rotor and stator in the pumping chamber, providing a limited level of vacuum. Whereas, two-stage rotary piston vacuum pumps consist of two sets of rotor and stator, which allows them to achieve a higher level of vacuum due to the decreased number of molecules in the second stage. These pumps are used in various industries like manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and electronics for different vacuum applications.</p></p>
