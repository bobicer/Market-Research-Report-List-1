<p><p>Expanded metal stair treads are stair steps made from expanded metal sheets that are designed for safety, traction, and durability. Expanded metal consists of a continuous sheet of metal that is cut and stretched to form diamond-shaped openings, creating a mesh-like texture. These stair treads are commonly used in industrial and commercial settings, such as factories, warehouses, and outdoor areas, to provide slip-resistant surfaces and enhance safety for people walking up and down the stairs.</p><p>The Expanded Metal Stair Treads Market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. The increasing focus on workplace safety and the stringent regulations imposed by various governments regarding worker safety are driving the demand for expanded metal stair treads. These treads are preferred over traditional options like concrete or wood due to their higher traction and slip resistance properties.</p><p>Additionally, the expanding industrial sector, especially in developing economies, is fueling the demand for expanded metal stair treads.
