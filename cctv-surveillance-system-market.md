<p><p>The CCTV Surveillance System market can be categorized into three types: Analog, Digital, and Network. Analog systems are the traditional setup where cameras capture footage and transmit it via coaxial cables to a DVR for storing and viewing. Digital systems, on the other hand, use IP cameras that capture and transmit high-resolution digital footage over an Ethernet network. Network systems leverage cloud technology, allowing cameras to connect directly to the internet, transmit footage, and store it remotely. These types differ in their means of capturing, transmitting, and storing surveillance footage.</p></p>
