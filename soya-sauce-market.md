<p><strong>The Soya Sauce Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brewed</li><li>Blended</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of soy sauce on the market: brewed soy sauce and blended soy sauce. Brewed soy sauce is made through a fermentation process that involves using soybeans, salt, wheat, and water. It has a more complex and rich flavor profile and is considered to be of higher quality. On the other hand, blended soy sauce is made by mixing brewed soy sauce with additives like artificial colors, flavors, and preservatives. It is generally cheaper but lacks the depth of flavor found in brewed soy sauce.</p></p>
