<p><p>Fuel injection systems have evolved over the years, with two main types dominating the market - Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) and Mechanical Fuel Injection System (MFI). EFI systems use electronic sensors and injectors to precisely control the fuel delivery to the engine, resulting in improved performance and efficiency. On the other hand, MFI systems rely on mechanical components to deliver fuel, offering simpler design and lower cost. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
