<p><ul><li>Carried-Up Type</li><li>Hang-Down Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cable suspension clamps are devices used to support and secure cables in various industries. There are two main types of cable suspension clamp markets: carried-up type and hang-down type. </p><p>Carried-up type suspension clamps are designed to elevate cables from the ground or any other surface. They are typically used in aerial cable installations or when cables need to be kept off the floor to prevent damage or interference.</p><p>Hang-down type suspension clamps, on the other hand, are used when cables need to be suspended from above. They are commonly employed in overhead power line installations to ensure proper cable alignment and prevent sagging.</p></p>
