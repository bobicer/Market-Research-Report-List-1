<p><p>Road cold recycler refers to a machine used in road construction to recycle existing asphalt pavement materials, reducing the need for new materials. The market types for road cold recyclers include asphalt emulsion recycler and foamed asphalt recycler. </p><p>An asphalt emulsion recycler utilizes an emulsion, a mixture of asphalt and water, to rejuvenate the old asphalt and bind it together. This process helps in creating a durable road surface. </p><p>On the other hand, a foamed asphalt recycler incorporates air and water into the asphalt mix, resulting in a lightweight foam that is used to restore the old pavement. This method provides good stability and reduces the overall project costs.</p></p>
