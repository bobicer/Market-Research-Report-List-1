Telescopic cranes have a hydraulic extension system that allows them to reach varying heights. Lattice cranes utilize a lattice structure for heavy-duty lifting tasks. Other types of offshore cranes include stiff boom, pedestal-mounted, and fixed boom cranes. Each type offers unique capabilities and is suited for different offshore applications, such as construction, oil and gas exploration, and wind turbine installation.</p></p>
