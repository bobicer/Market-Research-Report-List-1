<p><p>The turbidity analyzer market offers two main types of products: dry media and wet media analyzers. Dry media analyzers utilize solid-state or optical sensors to measure the clarity of a liquid or gas sample. They are suitable for applications with low sample volume and are known for their accuracy and stability. Wet media analyzers, on the other hand, use submerged sensors in liquid samples to measure turbidity. They are commonly used in industries that deal with large sample volumes and require regular cleaning and maintenance.</p></p>
