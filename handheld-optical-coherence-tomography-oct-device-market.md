<p><strong>The Handheld Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral-Domain OCT</li><li>Swept-Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Handheld Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Device market is divided into two types: Spectral-Domain OCT and Swept-Source OCT. Spectral-Domain OCT utilizes a broadband light source and a spectrometer, providing a high-speed imaging process and precise depth measurements. It is widely adopted due to its reliability and accuracy. On the other hand, Swept-Source OCT employs a tunable laser as the light source, offering superior depth range imaging capabilities. This type of OCT is particularly useful for imaging highly scattering tissues, making it popular in ophthalmology and cardiology applications.</p></p>
