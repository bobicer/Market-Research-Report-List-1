<p><p>Climbing gloves are available in two types: partial finger and full finger. Partial finger gloves cover only the palm area and leave the fingers partially exposed. They provide better dexterity and grip, making them suitable for tasks that require precise finger movements. On the other hand, full finger gloves cover the entire hand, including all the fingers. These gloves offer more protection from abrasions, cuts, and cold weather, making them ideal for climbing in harsh outdoor conditions. Each type has its own advantages and is selected based on the specific needs and preferences of climbers.</p></p>
