<p><strong>The Food Allergy Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymerase Chain reaction (PCR)</li><li>Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food allergy diagnostics market is classified into two types: Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) and Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA). PCR is a method used to amplify DNA samples and detect specific allergens present in food, allowing for accurate diagnosis of food allergies. ELISA, on the other hand, detects and measures the amount of specific antibodies produced in response to allergens in the blood. Both PCR and ELISA are widely used in food allergy diagnostics to identify and manage allergies effectively.</p></p>
