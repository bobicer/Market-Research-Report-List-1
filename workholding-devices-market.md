<p><p>Workholding devices play a crucial role in the manufacturing industry by securely holding workpieces during various machining operations. The milling/drilling workholding market offers devices like vises, clamps, and fixtures that provide stability and precision during milling and drilling processes. Turning workholding focuses on devices like chucks and collets, enabling secure rotation of workpieces during lathe operations. Grinding workholding includes magnetic chucks and fixtures that firmly hold workpieces during grinding operations. The EDM workholding market specializes in devices like electrodes and clamps, which securely hold workpieces during electrical discharge machining processes.</p></p>
